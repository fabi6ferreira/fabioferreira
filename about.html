Hey, I'm Aaron!

I'm a Front End Developer based in Leigh-on-Sea, UK. Passionate about building web applications that are performant, accessible and have a great user experience. Outside of work I love walking our cocker spaniel (she has grown a bit since here), going to coffee shops, watching Formula 1 and being outdoors.
